About This Project
Project Overview

BoilerCOL aims to serve as a reliable, comprehensive, all-encompassing digital database that those of Purdue University and the school’s periphery communities can consult to gain a deeper understanding of market conditions and other relevant financial information in Greater Lafayette.

BoilerCOL confronts the sometimes anecdotal and informal nature of discussion surrounding affordability on college campuses like Purdue’s. Among the core focuses of this initiative is the inequity that naturally arises in the situations of international students—a large, vibrant, and influential cohort at Purdue in particular—who often do not have the same market intuition as in-state and/or domestic students. BoilerCOL’s mission is to level the playing field in situations like this, to make sure that all Purdue students are equipped to make the best financial decisions for themselves.

In 2026, the affordability crisis commands more of the public eye than ever. College campuses are particularly vulnerable, as, historically, the rise of inflation rates in college towns has outpaced the national average. To many, a dollar on campus seems to stretch less and less every semester. This project seeks to give Boilers a relevant advantage in navigating this increasingly distressing crisis.
