About This Project
Project Overview

BoilerCOL EDIT THIS SECTION is a student-driven research initiative dedicated to tracking and analyzing the cost of living in the Greater Lafayette area. By aggregating publicly available rental listings, utility estimates, and local pricing data, we build a comprehensive picture of what it actually costs to live near Purdue University.

Our Consumer Price Index methodology draws on a standardized basket of goods and services — including housing, transportation, food, and utilities — to produce a single, comparable inflation figure for West Lafayette. The 4.94% year-over-year rate reflects changes in that basket relative to the prior measurement period.

This dashboard is intended as a resource for students, families, and university administrators seeking to understand local affordability trends. All data is collected and processed independently; figures should be interpreted alongside national benchmarks and official BLS statistics for full context.
