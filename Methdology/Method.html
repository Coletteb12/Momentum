BoilerCOL Data Collection and Organization
Behind the Data
Methodology
Data collection, cleaning, and organization for the BoilerCOL cost-of-living guide.
📋
Data Collection
Primary Data
  • Student survey — housing, groceries, transportation, misc. costs
Market Data
  • Housing listings — Zillow, Apartments.com, Redfin, Purdue housing
  • Grocery prices — standardized basket across local stores
  • Transportation — parking, gas, bus systems
Supplementary
  • Insurance estimates (limited sample)
📅
Collection Approach
  • 2-week fixed data collection window
  • Decentralized team input → broad coverage of cost factors
  • Focus on student-relevant, real-world expenses
🧹
Data Cleaning & Standardization
  • Converted all costs to monthly estimates
  • Removed duplicates and reviewed outliers
  • Organized into categories: Housing | Groceries | Transportation
🗃️
Data Organization
  • Centralized in GitHub repository
⚠️
Transparency & Limitations
  • Fully documented sources and methods
  • Reproducible data pipeline
Limitations
  • Short time window
  • Self-reported survey bias
  • Manual data inconsistencies
