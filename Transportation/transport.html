🚌 Bus Pass — Included Apartments

Several off-campus apartments near Purdue include a CityBus pass in your rent, giving you free unlimited access to West Lafayette transit. This effectively eliminates the need for a car for grocery runs, campus commutes, and errands — and it's already factored into your lease price.

Village West
~3.1 mi from campus · Salisbury St
🚌 Bus Pass Included In Rent
Unlimited CityBus access bundled with lease. Routes 1, 4, and 6 service this corridor with stops near Walmart and Meijer on Sagamore Pkwy.
Lark
~4.3 mi from campus · Northwestern Ave
🚌 Bus Pass Included In Rent
CityBus pass included for all residents. Close proximity to campus means most errands can be done on foot or by bus — no parking permit needed.
Alight West Lafayette
~4.3 mi from campus · Littleton St
🚌 Bus Pass Included In Rent
Full CityBus pass included with every unit. The Route 4 stop is directly outside, giving direct access to campus and the Sagamore Pkwy grocery corridor.
Redpoint
~4.8 mi from campus · Yeager Rd
🚌 Bus Pass Included In Rent
CityBus pass bundled into lease terms for all residents. Routes serving Walmart on Creasy Lane and Target on Sagamore Pkwy are accessible without transfers.
💡 What does this save you?  A Purdue student CityBus pass costs ~$75–$100/semester if purchased separately (~$150–$200/year). Apartments with included passes effectively reduce your living costs by that amount — and if it means you don't need a car on campus, your savings on gas, parking permits (~$450–$600/yr at Purdue), and car maintenance are significantly higher.
